Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 24, 2024

Welcome to the Sadguru Mauli app, a mobile application that provides users with religious content. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your experience with our app is safe. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when using our app.

Information Collection and Use

The Sadguru Mauli app does not collect, store, or share any personal information, such as your name, email, phone number, or other identifiable data. Our app is designed to display static religious text and images. However, we use Firebase services to upload and download images that are displayed within the app.

Only administrators with a valid admin password can upload images, while all users can view the downloaded images within the app interface. The images are not saved to the user’s device gallery.

Permissions

To provide functionality, the Sadguru Mauli app requests the following permissions:

  • Internet Permission: Required to upload and download images using Firebase services.
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES Permission: Used to access images if necessary for administrative functions. This permission is not utilized for general users.

Third-Party Services

The Sadguru Mauli app integrates Firebase services for image upload and download functionality. Firebase is a service provided by Google LLC. By using our app, you agree to Firebase’s Privacy Policy. For more information, visit Firebase Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not collect any data from children or adults alike. The app is designed to ensure a safe and secure experience for all users.
